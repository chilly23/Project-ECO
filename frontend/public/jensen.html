Jensen Huang co-founded NVIDIA in 1993 and has served as its CEO since its inception, steering the company from a niche graphics card maker to a global leader in GPU technology, AI, and data‑center solutions. Under his leadership, NVIDIA pioneered real‑time ray tracing, deep learning frameworks, and expanded into automotive and edge computing, driving significant market growth and innovation. Huang is widely recognized for his visionary approach, frequent keynote speeches, and commitment to advancing technology for societal benefit.
